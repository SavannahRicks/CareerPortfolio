My Education

I am a Graduate of Penn State Erie, The Behrend College and I studied Digital Media, Arts & Technology (DIGIT) and minored in Writing & Digital Media. When I first started at Penn State, I was initially a Computer Science major. After a year, I realized that Computer Science was not for me. I enjoyed the coding aspect, but I felt like I needed to visually see the creations I was making. When I found that the DIGIT major had both coding and arts, I knew I had to switch majors, and I am so glad I did! Building websites is one of my favorite things to do now; I love how there is problem solving, instant results, and a mix of art. Because the languages of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc. are always changing, there is always something to learn! I know that I do not know everything about web dev, but I am always looking for something new to expand my knowledge.

Marketing Intern Experience

In May 2025, I began my internship with Country Fair. My experience with them has been incredibly exciting and knowledgeable. Working alongside my supervisor and another intern, we focused on marketing events at Country Fair Stores across PA and neighboring states. These events offered food and prizes to new and current country fair customers to show our gratitude to our customers. We would also promote the company’s loyalty program with the goal of reaching 30 percent usage company wide. By August 2025 we successfully hit that goal! In between event days, we’d all split off to visit a couple stores a day, where we’d advertise the loyalty program on our own. During which, I would ask the store workers of any comments, questions or concerns regarding the program. Throughout the summer, I have been taking in technical, user and quality complaints. Throughout these complaints I noticed a consistent issue that had been recurring throughout most of the stores we interacted with. With this I had notified my higher-ups of the issue, resulting in the improvement of the overall quality control steps that were taken going forward.
